The Sassy Spoon

Pune Is a Modern Indian restaurant located in Koregaon Park

The Sassy Spoon Pune is a modern Indian restaurant located in Koregaon Park, Pune. It offers a wide variety of Indian dishes, including vegetarian and gluten-free options. The restaurant has a casual and inviting atmosphere, and it is a popular spot for both locals and tourists.

Nestled in the heart of Pune, The Sassy spoon is a culinary haven born from a passion for authentic flavors and warm hospitality. Our journey began in 1980 when Rachel Goenka, a seasoned chef with a love for Cuisine , dreamt of creating a space where guests could savor the rich tapestry of india's culinary heritage.
